Programming Chess in C

Fall, 2023

This was for the final project of EE 285, which is a class on C progamming. It was a group project between me, my lab partner Olivia, and another group of lab partners: Rachel and Dede. We got to choose what to make for our project so we chose the relatively ambitious goal of making a fully functional chess game. In the end we far exceeded all of our goals and we made the highest scoring project in the class.
